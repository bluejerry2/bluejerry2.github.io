This is a personal archive of prose I’ve chosen to make public. Each piece includes the Korean original and an English translation.

I live in an English-speaking country. However, when I write, Korean is often the more comfortable tool, so most pieces start in Korean. This is simply a matter of familiarity, not a statement about valuing Korean over English (or vice versa).

2026-03-02. For me, prose that contains a slight element of fiction is nothing more and nothing less than a thought experiment—an exercise through which I renew what I believe to be the freedom given to me. In that sense, I have no interest in commercial publication or in becoming well known. I do not consider myself intellectually strong enough to think freely within the logic of capital, and for that reason I choose to distance myself from those seemingly admirable values. At the same time, I cannot completely abandon the desire to reach others, which is why I created this website. For the same reason, its design prioritizes readability above all else.
